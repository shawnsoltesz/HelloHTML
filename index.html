Kayaking Adventures Around Florida

In 2019 I was taken to Weeki Wachee to kayak for the day and I was not only mesmerized, but I was really interested in kayaking more. What better way to get a glimpse within nature that can only be experienced on water? That day on the water, while a two seater tandem kayak was a fun first kayaking, I wanted to give it a whirl solo. Not long after, a friend came to visit and I took him to Weeki Wachee. We both paddled solo and I quickly realized that this activity could be a lot of fun while enjoying Florida's weather and surroundings.

After a few more kayaking adventures and rental costs adding up, it dawned on my friend and I to buy our own kayaks! And that we did on July 4th, 2019. Dick’s Sporting Goods in Tampa was having a sale and we came home with kayaks, life vests and whistle, and two paddles each. We were ready to explore the waterways St Pete had to offer, on our own terms. Leading up to making the purchase, I did some research to better understand kayaks beyond the offerings the rental services offer. I’ll share some of that information below along with a few highlights from local places to enjoy.
